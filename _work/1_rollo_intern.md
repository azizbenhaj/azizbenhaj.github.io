I worked as a Data Engineer at **Rollomatic SA**, a Swiss industrial company specialized in high-precision CNC machines for tool grinding and laser machining. The company operates in a data-rich manufacturing environment where machine learning and data engineering are increasingly used to improve engineering workflows.

---

## 🧑‍💻 Role Overview

During this **7-month internship**, I worked as a Data Engineer Intern, focusing on building and supporting data infrastructure for industrial machine learning applications.

---

## ⚙️ Responsibilities

- Development of data pipelines for industrial tool datasets  
- Processing structured tool parameters and geometric data  
- Cleaning and organizing manufacturing datasets for ML use  
- Supporting dataset preparation for similarity-based retrieval systems  
- Collaborating with engineers to align data formats with production requirements  

---

## 🧠 Key Contributions

- Contributed to the foundation of a **hybrid similarity search system**
- Combined:
  - Structured parameter-based similarity
  - Early-stage visual feature extraction for tool geometry
- Helped establish initial data workflows for industrial ML experimentation  

---

## 📌 Outcome

This role provided strong experience in:
- Real-world data engineering in an industrial setting  
- Preparing data for machine learning systems  
- Understanding constraints of manufacturing workflows
